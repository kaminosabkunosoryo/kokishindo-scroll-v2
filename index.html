Prologue — The Curious Way

In 2017 I founded Kokishin Ryu — the Way of the Curious Heart, a joyful path of inquiry.

More than martial art, Kokishin is a system of spiritual resilience, born from sorrow, shaped by light.

Chapter I — The Wounded Seed

I was raised in a house of light and shadow. Trauma became the forge; love, the tempering.

Chapter II — The Two Teachers

Training under Brian Stewart and Dr. A.J. “Sasha” Burns (1985‑1991) revealed balance and forged my own path.
